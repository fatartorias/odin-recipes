Mexican Red Rice

Mexican Red Rice recipe

This is the Mexican Red Rice plate.

Found throughout the country in households and restaurants, it is a staple of Mexican food.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Cooking oil
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Peas
  • Corn kernels
  • Carrot
  • Potato

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice with cold water.
  2. Allow the rice to dry.
  3. Fry the rice at a low fire setting.
  4. Liquefy the tomato, onion, and garlic.
  5. Dice the carrots and potatoes.
  6. Add two cups of water for every cup of rice to the fried rice.
  7. Add the vegetables and the liquefied sauce along with a teaspoon of salt to the mix.
  8. Bring to boil and then cook at low fire for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy with innumerable dishes.
