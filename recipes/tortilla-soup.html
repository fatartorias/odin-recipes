Tortilla Soup

Tortilla Soup recipe

Another easy to make, tasty mexican dish.

You'll never have enough of this one.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Chicken stock
  • Tortillas
  • Avocados
  • Green chillies
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Suggested cheese: manchego, quesillo, panela
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken breast with onion, garlic and salt.
  2. Cut the tortillas into half inch squares or quarter inch thick strips.
  3. Chop the avocados, cheese if applicable, and green chillies.
  4. Liquefy the tomato, onion and the garlic.
  5. Cook this sauce with some of the chicken stock.
  6. Fry the tortillas until they are crispy.
  7. Serve the sauce with the tortilla chips and add chillies, avocado, cream and cheese to your liking.
  8. Enjoy.
